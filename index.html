Our Story

Time flies and after sharing nearly ten years together we are excited to tie the knot in front of our closest friends and family!

Our story is a long one with no shortage of love and adventure but here's the brief history of Panda (Chris) and Bubba (Tracey) — or "Pandabub" as our friends often call us.

Love at First Sight

The two native Angelinos first crossed paths in 2007. Chris fell smitten with the cute girl in scrubs and found the way to her heart over burgers, Diddy Riese cookie sandwiches and witty humor. Many years and an impressive list of inside jokes later, the two became Pandabub.

Home Sweet Home

For Tracey, it was a quick summer between undergrad and grad school. Knowing he would never see her otherwise, they decided to take a leap of faith and moved into their Westwood apartment in 2010. 3 years of cohabitation bliss later they moved into their first home after Tracey earned her doctorate, finally becoming his "Dr. Bubs."

Happily Ever After

Now that there was finally time to indulge in their travel addiction (and Tracey’s extensive bucket list), they found themselves on countless adventures across the country and globe. For many years Chris would listen to her reminisce about summers spent camping at her favorite place, Yosemite National Park, and how she was determined to accomplish her childhood dream of climbing up Half Dome. Wanting him to experience it her way, they embarked on a 3-day backpacking trip which included the infamous chains to Half Dome summit. Little did she know the trail lead straight up to the proposal of her dreams, catching her completely off guard. The newly engaged duo and “rock” made it safely back down the mountain to continue living happily ever after.
