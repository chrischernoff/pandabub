Our Story

Time sure does fly, and after sharing nearly ten years together we are excited to tie the knot in front of our closest friends and family.

Our story is a long one, filled with excitement and lots of love, but here's a brief history of Panda (Chris) and Bubba (Tracey) — or "Pandabub" as our friends often call us.

Love at First Sight

Both native Los Angelinos, we met in 2007 while we were in school and hit it off instantly. Tracey was beginning her journey to becoming a Physical Therapist at Santa Monica College and later Cal State Long Beach, while Chris was studying design at UCLA. It didn't take long for things to become official and we've become pretty much inseparable ever since!

Home Sweet Home

After determining that we weren't quite sick of each other yet, we moved in together in 2010 as Tracey began grad school at Mount St. Mary's University. We'd later go on to buy our first home together after Tracey earned her doctorate, thus becoming "Dr. Bubs."

Happily Ever After

After countless adventures across the country and around the globe, Chris finally popped the big question in Tracey's favorite place on Earth: Yosemite National Park. After a three-day backpacking trip, the proposal happened at the summit of the famous "Half Dome."
